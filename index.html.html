I am not a schoolboy known for discipline problems or known for ingenuity but I am just a normal schoolboy. at the end of the current year in form 1 school where I was still a schoolboy who had just moved on from the primary school phase and when entering secondary school I would be a bit of a boyish boy. I remember that time I just finished the art class session and that time I learned to color stones. I threw the stone and it wasn't even about the two seniors but they looked at us with dissatisfied faces. A few days later we were called to the discipline room without knowing our offense.
